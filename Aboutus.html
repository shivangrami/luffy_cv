About:

My Journey to Become the Pirate King:

The Beginning (East Blue Saga):

I set out from my hometown of Fushia Village with the dream of becoming the Pirate King. I ate the Gum-Gum Fruit, gaining the ability to stretch my body like rubber but losing the ability to swim. I recruited my first crew member, Zoro, who became the crew's swordsman.


Seeking Companions (Recruitment Arcs):

My journey continued as I recruited Nami, the crew's navigator, who had a troubled past with the Arlong Pirates. I added Usopp, the sharpshooter and storyteller, to my crew after an adventure in Syrup Village. I met Sanji, a skilled chef and fighter, while facing the powerful Baratie restaurant.


The Grand Line (Grand Line Saga):

My growing crew and I entered the treacherous Grand Line, determined to find the legendary One Piece. We encountered foes like Crocodile and the Baroque Works organization, with me defeating Crocodile in Alabasta. I learned about the Seven Warlords of the Sea and the World Government's power.


The Enies Lobby and Water 7 Arcs:

I faced internal conflict when Robin left the crew, and we journeyed to Water 7 to rescue her. The crew confronted the CP9 and fought our way through Enies Lobby to save Robin. My determination to protect my friends grew stronger.


The Thriller Bark and Sabaody Archipelago Arcs:

My crew and I encountered the ghostly Thriller Bark and its bizarre inhabitants, including Gecko Moria. We reached Sabaody Archipelago, where we encountered the Celestial Dragons and the worst generation of pirates. A clash with Admiral Kizaru led to our separation.


The Summit War Saga:

I took part in the Battle of Marineford to save my brother, Ace, leading to a tragic loss. The crew suffered another separation as we went our separate ways after Ace's death. My resolve to become Pirate King remained unshaken.


The Time Skip:

My crew and I spent two years apart, training to become stronger.


The New World (Punk Hazard, Dressrosa, and Whole Cake Island Arcs):

The Straw Hat Pirates reunited and entered the New World. We faced formidable foes like Doflamingo, Big Mom, and their crews. I continued to grow as a pirate and leader.


Wano Country Arc:

My crew and I entered Wano Country to support the samurai in their rebellion against Kaido and the Beast Pirates. We uncovered the secrets of the Road Poneglyphs and the history of the world.


The Road to One Piece:

My ultimate goal is to reach Laugh Tale (formerly Raftel) and find One Piece, the ultimate treasure. I faced new challenges, allied with powerful individuals, and continued my journey to become the Pirate King.
